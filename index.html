Data Sources & Methodology
  • Primary Sources: Macroeconomic data (GDP, Growth, Exchange Rates) from MoSPI, RBI, and the World Bank. State-level FDI from DPIIT. Market Capitalization from NSE historical records, and Unemployment estimates from harmonized PLFS / CMIE / ILO data.
  • Geopolitical History: Data for Maharashtra and Gujarat prior to the May 1960 Bombay Reorganisation Act are intentionally treated as `null` to respect historical geopolitical boundaries.
  • Real GDP & Deflator Structuring: Real GDP relies entirely on official historical spliced series data. The newly added Deflator metric accurately reflects the implied inflation via the ratio of Nominal to Real volume.
  • Provisional Tracking: Fiscal years 2023-24 (FY24) and beyond are currently marked as provisional estimates or forecasts and are styled with dashed trend lines.
